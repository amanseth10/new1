About Sparrow...

A sparrow is a member of the genus Passer. They are small passerine birds which belong to the family Passeridae. They are also known as old-world sparrows. Sparrows often make their nests near houses or buildings. This means they are one of the easiest birds to see in the wild. The genus has about 30 species around the world. The best known of these is the house sparrow, Passer domesticus.
